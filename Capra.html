The Craft

Our dough starts with wheat sourdough and time — long fermentation, no shortcuts. It bakes fast in a very hot oven, just long enough for the leopard spots to appear on the crust. Soft at the edges, perfectly leavened, easy to digest.

The tomatoes are San Marzano DOP. The mozzarella is Fior di Latte. The toppings are few and intentional — goat cheese cream with cranberries and sage, bresaola with lemon zest, cacio e pepe with plum balsamic. We keep the menu small because every pizza on it has earned its place.

Our pizzaiolo Felice is a real Neapolitan. He worked in London, the Netherlands, and for a sheikh before choosing Leipzig. The sourdough gets his time and attention. The result is on your plate.

— Capra, since November 2022

Our Beer

We don’t just make pizza

Weiße Elster Pale Ale is ours, brewed right here in Leipzig. It pours on tap alongside a classic Pilsner.

For wine lovers, a curated list of classic and natural wines. For everyone else, regional Schorlen, Crodino, and a proper range of alcohol-free options.
